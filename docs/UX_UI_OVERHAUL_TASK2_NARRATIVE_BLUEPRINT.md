# UX/UI Overhaul - Task 2/8

Narrative and copy blueprint for:

**The Path from Data to Intelligence**

## Narrative Positioning

I build intelligent systems that connect technical model performance with business and economic value.  
My edge is combining quantitative economics, computer vision, and production-oriented data engineering.

## Audience

- Hiring managers for AI/Data Engineer roles
- Product and strategy teams looking for applied ML outcomes
- Technical peers evaluating depth in CV/ML and data systems

## Core Messaging Pillars

1. **Data Discipline**: structured pipelines turn noisy signals into reliable inputs.
2. **Model Intelligence**: CV/ML solutions are selected and validated for real constraints.
3. **Economic Impact**: decisions are measured in risk reduction, efficiency, and ROI.
4. **Execution Quality**: reproducible workflows from exploration to deployment.
